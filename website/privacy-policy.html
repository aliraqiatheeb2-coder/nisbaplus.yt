Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 28, 2025

Nisba+ ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our productivity application, Nisba+.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We do not collect any personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it to us. Personal information may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Account credentials (if you choose to create an account)

Usage Data

We may collect information automatically when you use the application, including:

  • Device information (device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers)
  • Usage statistics (features used, session duration, interaction patterns)
  • Performance data (app crashes, loading times, error logs)
  • Diagnostic information to help improve app stability

Habit and Task Data

Habit tracking, task lists, productivity data, and all user-generated content you enter into the application are stored locally on your device. We do not collect or store this information on our servers. Your data belongs to you and remains private.

2. Use of Your Information

We may use information we collect for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the application functionality
  • To improve and personalize your user experience
  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize app performance
  • To communicate with you, including responding to inquiries and providing support
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security concerns
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights
  • To send you important updates about the application (infrequent)

3. Data Protection

Local Storage

All your habit tracking, task management, and productivity data are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store this information on external servers. You have full control over your data.

Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data transmission when communicating with third-party services
  • Secure coding practices to prevent vulnerabilities
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls to limit who can view support requests

Data Retention

We retain support request information for up to 2 years for quality assurance and legal compliance purposes. You may request deletion of your support data at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. However, we may use third-party services that collect information automatically:

Analytics Services

We may use analytics services to understand how users interact with our application:

  • Google Analytics for Firebase - Provides anonymous usage statistics and crash reporting to improve app stability and user experience

Cloud Services

If you choose to use cloud backup features (optional):

  • Google Drive API - For cloud backup and synchronization (only if explicitly enabled by user)

Advertising

We do not display advertisements in our application and do not share data with advertising networks.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your personal information.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request limitation of how we process your personal information.
  • Right to Data Portability: You can request transfer of your personal information to another party.
  • Right to Object: You can object to our processing of your personal information.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw your consent for data processing at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@nisbaplus.yt.

6. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information from our files.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

9. Data Processing Agreement

We act as a data controller for the limited personal information we collect. We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk.

Data Processing Purposes

  • App functionality and performance optimization
  • User support and communication
  • Legal compliance and security
  • Research and development (anonymized data only)

International Data Transfers

If we transfer your information outside of your country, we will ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as standard contractual clauses approved by relevant authorities.
